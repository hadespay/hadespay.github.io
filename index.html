Frequently Asked Questions

What tokens are listed in the Token dropdown?

We support all SPL tokens.
The token list is created from the user’s wallet. When you connect to the app with your wallet, the app fetches the token list from your wallet and lists them in the app to make it easier for you to create the stream.

What is Release Funds?

Release Funds is the interval at which you want to release the tokens to the recipient. For example, if you select Monthly, then the desired tokens will be released every month to the recipient.

What is Duration?

Duration is the entire period for which you want to stream the tokens. This is the time for which the stream will run.

What is the difference between Release Funds and Duration?

Release Funds is the interval at which you want to release the tokens to the recipient, whereas Duration is the entire period for which you want to stream the tokens.
For example, if you select Monthly in Release Funds and 1 Year in Duration, then the desired tokens will be released every month (at the rate specified) to the recipient, for an entire year.
For calculation purposes, below is true for both Release Funds and Duration:
1 Month = 30 Days
1 Year = 365 Days

What is Rate?

Rate is the amount that will be available for withdrawal at the end of every interval. It is calculated based on the total deposit, interval and duration of the stream.

Difference between Definite Stream and Continuous Stream?

A Definite Stream has a fixed Start and End date and it cannot be reloaded with any more amount than originally set, whereas a Continuous Stream doesn’t have a fixed End date and can be continually extended by reloading it with more amount.

Advanced Settings Options?

There are multiple controls in Advanced Settings. You can choose who will have the authority to withdraw tokens from the stream and also to perform various actions like Cancelling, Pausing and Resuming the stream.

What is Amount?

Amount is the total deposit that will be locked into the stream for the period set in duration.
